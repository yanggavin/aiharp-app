Quick start

How to use the app

These steps are written for a new user who wants to go from install to a full practice run without hunting through menus.

Step 1

Complete setup

  1. Open the app and allow microphone access.
  2. On the Home tab, tap Start Practice or open the Practice tab directly.
  3. If microphone permission is denied, the app can still run in demo feedback mode.
Step 2

Run a practice session

  1. Choose a category such as Single Notes, Scales, Rhythm, or Patterns.
  2. Tap Start on an exercise card.
  3. Watch the harmonica hole bar, beat lane, and pitch ring while you play.
  4. Tap Finish when you want the final score and coaching summary.
Step 3

Use feedback effectively

Pitch Use the ring color and cents offset to stabilize intonation.
Rhythm Match the beat lane and enable the metronome when timing slips.
Stability Focus on clean breath control and smooth note transitions.
Local AI

Understand the AI features

The app includes two optional on-device AI features: live audio scene detection and a post-session AI coach. These features use Apple system frameworks on supported devices and do not upload your raw practice audio.

  • Audio Scene Detection: warns you about background speech, music, or environmental noise.
  • AI Coach: generates a short practice note after you finish a run.
Tabs

Import and export tabs

Go to the Songs tab to browse built-in drills or bring in your own tab files. Import and export are included with the paid app download.

  • Import from the top-right toolbar on the Songs screen.
  • Export from the song detail screen.
  • Tabs import and export are included in the paid app download.
Troubleshooting

Common issues

  • The app cannot hear my harmonica: check microphone permission, lower ambient noise, and raise your note clarity.
  • The metronome feels too loud or soft: adjust your device output volume before practice.
  • AI coach says it is unavailable: Apple Intelligence or device eligibility may not be ready on your device.
  • Imported tabs are missing: confirm the file imported successfully on the Songs screen and reopen the app if the list has not refreshed yet.

If you contact support, include your device model, iOS version, the app version, and a short description of what you tried. That cuts down the back-and-forth.

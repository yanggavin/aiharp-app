Privacy

Your practice audio is processed on-device.

This policy explains what AI Harp stores, what it does not collect, and how the app handles optional features such as tab import/export and on-device AI coaching.

Effective date: March 10, 2026 Controller contact: support@aiharp-app.newtree.tech
Overview

1. What we collect

The app is designed to keep your practice data local to your device whenever possible. We do not run a cloud account system inside the app.

  • Microphone input: used for live harmonica feedback and processed on-device.
  • Practice history: session scores, duration, and hole accuracy are stored locally on your device.
  • Imported tabs: tab files you import are stored locally so they remain available after relaunch.
  • Support emails: if you email us, we receive the information you include in that message.
Audio

2. How microphone audio is used

Microphone audio is used for pitch detection, hole guidance, timing feedback, and optional on-device audio scene analysis. Raw practice audio is not uploaded by the app for routine use.

  • The app requires microphone permission for live practice feedback.
  • If permission is denied, the app can still operate in a demo mode.
  • Optional AI features use Apple system frameworks on supported devices.
AI

3. On-device AI features

The app can use Apple-provided on-device frameworks for audio scene detection and short coaching summaries. Availability depends on device, operating system, locale, and Apple Intelligence readiness.

  • Audio scene detection helps identify background music, speech, or environmental noise.
  • The AI coach generates a short note after a practice run using on-device context.
  • These features can be disabled in Settings.
Storage

4. Local storage and purchases

The app stores settings, imported tabs, and practice progress on your device. App download billing is handled by the App Store.

  • We do not create a separate in-app user account.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not include third-party advertising SDKs in the app.
Sharing

5. When information may be shared

We only receive information that you voluntarily provide through support communication or platform purchase flows. Payment for the app purchase is handled by Apple, not by us directly.

Your rights

6. Your choices

  • You can disable AI audio scene detection and AI coach features in Settings.
  • You can reset practice progress inside the app.
  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored data from your device.
Children

7. Children’s privacy

AI Harp is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children through an in-app account system. Parents or guardians may contact us if they have questions about support correspondence.

Changes

8. Policy updates

We may update this policy when app features or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when revisions are published.

Contact

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@aiharp-app.newtree.tech.
